Monnat Living — Logo Concepts

Three distinct directions for review · Tagline: "Made for living"

V1 · The Leaf

Botanical
Direction: Warm, natural, grounded. A minimalist olive-inspired botanical mark paired with a refined serif wordmark. The leaf sprig connects to the brand's natural, earthy palette (sage + terracotta). Best for lifestyle-forward contexts.

V2 · The Monogram

Architectural
Direction: Bold, architectural, confident. A custom geometric "M" monogram framed in a warm arch — like a doorway into a home. The monogram can stand alone as a favicon or social avatar. Best for brands that need a recognizable symbol.

V3 · The Stack

Editorial
Direction: Editorial, minimal, distinctive. An oversized "M" as the hero letter with "ONNAT" and "IVING" separated beside it by a thin terracotta line. It reads as a cohesive word but creates a striking visual signature. Best for fashion/design-forward contexts.
